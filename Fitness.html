Life is an Adventure: Let's Explore!

Rhode Island is the smallest of the States but offers a wide array of places to hike, walk, or bike. It also has some tremendously beautiful sites whether it be by the ocean, in the rolling hills, or in the middle of the city.

There are many scenic locales that can be a perfect match for your chosen activities;let RIPTA take you there,and you will be helping the environment as well.
